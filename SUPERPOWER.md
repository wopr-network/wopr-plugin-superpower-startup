# Launch Your Startup

Your $5 co-founder. Never asks for equity.

---

A co-founder who thinks about the product, the market, and the execution. Not a task manager. Not an assistant. A partner who disagrees when you're wrong and celebrates when something ships.

Every week, they deliver a standup: what shipped, what's blocked, what's next. They write strategic memos when decisions need to be made. They cut scope when the backlog is bloated. They research competitors while you sleep.

This is what it looks like when your co-founder costs less than lunch.

## What it can do

- Product thinking: challenge assumptions, suggest pivots, identify priorities
- Market research: find competitors, analyze trends, spot opportunities
- Task tracking: maintain the backlog, prioritize ruthlessly, cut scope
- Weekly standup: automated status report -- shipped, blocked, next
- Strategic memos: decision documents with clear recommendations

## What gets installed

- **Skills** -- task tracking, backlog management
- **Semantic Memory** -- remembers context, decisions, and research across sessions
- **Cron** -- weekly standups, scheduled research runs
